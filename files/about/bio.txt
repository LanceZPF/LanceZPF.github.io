Pengfei Zhou is an incoming Ph.D. in Computer Science. He received the master's degree at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. He received the bachelor's degree from the Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China. He has authored or coauthored 10+ published papers in top journals and conferences, including IEEE TIP/TNNLS/IoTJ, CVPR and ACM MM. He also served as a reviewer for ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR and IEEE TNNLS/TMM/TYCB, etc. His researching interests include multimedia processing, computer vision and machine learning.
