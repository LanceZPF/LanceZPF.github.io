Pengfei Zhou is an incoming Ph.D. in Computer Science. He received the master's degree at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. He received the bachelor's degree from the Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China. He has authored or coauthored 10 published papers in relevant journals and conferences, including IEEE TIP/ TNNLS/IoTJ and ACM MM. He also served as a reviewer for NeurIPS 2024, ACM MM 2024/2023 and IEEE TNNLS/TMM/TYCB, etc. His researching interests include multimedia processing and analysis, computer vision and machine learning.
