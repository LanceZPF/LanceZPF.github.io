Pengfei Zhou is currently pursuing the master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Information Processing, Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. He received the bachelor's degree in Internet of Things Engineering from the Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in 2021. He has authored or coauthored 9 published papers in relevant journals and conferences, including IEEE TNNLS, IEEE IoTJ, and ACM MM. He also served as a reviewer for IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TMM and ACM MM2023, etc. His researching interests include food computing, multimedia processing and computer vision.
