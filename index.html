Pengfei Zhou

I am pursuing the Ph.D. degree in School of Computing at National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Yang You and funded by NUS Research Scholarship.

I received my M.E. degree at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was advised by Prof. Weiqing Min and Prof. Shuqiang Jiang and worked in VIPL Lab, Key Lab of Intelligent Information Processing and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my B.E. degree from Zhejiang University of Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Cong Bai.

I have been honored with the Outstanding Graduate Award from both Beijing and Zhejiang, as well as the China National Scholarship on two occasions. Additionally, I secured second place at ImageCLEFlifelog2019 LMRT task. I was very glad to work with Kaipeng Zhang at Shanghai AI Laboratory.

Research Interests

I am currently working on research in multimodal intelligence, computer vision, and machine learning, especially focusing on Multimodal Large Language Models and Multimodal Generation. I am also interested in AI-enabled healthcare.
